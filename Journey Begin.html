Gender Equality
and
Women Empowerment

➣The term Gender equality means, all genders are free to pursue whatever career, lifestyle choice, and abilities they want without discrimination.

➣The term Women Empowerment means to promoting women’s sense of self-worth, their ability to determine their own choices, and their right to influence social change for themselves and others.

Purpose


➣The purpose of Gender Equality and Women Empowerment advocacy, is to build stronger rights for gender equality and women’s empowerment.


Why Matters ?


➣Equality really matters to us, it is a fundamental human right, as well as a prerequisite for a stable and harmonious world. Let's go for equality to see the beauty of life.
